Haitong Zheng

Graphic Design

insta: helloheyhowareyou


I observe to create. It's always interesting to put myself in a observer position while designing.
Tbh, sometimes I wonder if that is my escape from all the negativity. However, I do enjoy balancing the humor and the tiny absurdity between my works and this bigger context.

Mostly focus on prints, I explore other media such as animation, coding and photography. Popular culture, trends and current social issues are three major themes in all of my works.
